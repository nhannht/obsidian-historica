# Showcase

Screenshots of the Historica marketing site (`website/`), captured from the
production build at 1440px (2x) and 390px (3x) on 2026-07-20.

- `website-hero.png` - desktop hero: liquid-glass nav pill, headline, and the
  macOS-style window rendering prose becoming a timeline (Int UI dark mock).
- `website-features.png` - the 3x2 feature bento over the WebGL lightning
  background.
- `website-mobile.png` - 390px mobile view with the hamburger nav sheet open
  (paper sheet under the glass pill).

The social preview image lives at `website/public/og.png` (1200x630) and is
wired via `og:image` / `twitter:card` in `website/index.html`.
