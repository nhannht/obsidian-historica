# Showcase

## Plugin (the product)

Screenshots of Historica running inside Obsidian (dark theme, WW2 demo note),
captured at 1440px (2x) on 2026-07-20, downscaled to 1920w.

- `plugin-timeline.png` - the money shot: prose paragraphs above, the rendered
  `historica` block below - toolbar (12 dates, 12 sentences, 100% coverage,
  Saved), minimap brush 1938-1946, and the 12-entry spine with date chips.
- `plugin-entry-cards.png` - "Expand all" state: entry cards with date chip,
  significance slider (1-5), full sentence, and the "Add a note" annotation
  field.
- `plugin-context-menu.png` - per-entry right-click menu on an expanded card:
  Jump to source, Add attachment, Hide from view, Mark as anchor, Dismiss
  extraction.
