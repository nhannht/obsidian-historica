# My Life Story

I was born on March 15, 1990 in a small town. Life was simple.

On June 3, 2005, I started high school. It changed everything.

By September 2010, I had enrolled at university, studying computer science.

January 12, 2014 was the day I got my first real job at a startup.

On April 20, 2017, I got married. Best day ever.

November 2019 brought our first child into the world.

March 15, 2023 — my company got acquired. Bittersweet.
